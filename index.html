OUR MISSION & VISION
Driven by Mission
At USV, our mission is clear. We aim to be a reliable provider of healthcare products and services, not just within India, but the world over. We strive to deliver superior value to our consumers and stakeholders through specialised and focused product innovation. Our work environment is designed to value people, foster learning, nurture creativity, and drive collective excellence.
Led by Vision
We see a future where we’ve achieved leadership in all our business units, earning the respect of patients, physicians, and stakeholders alike. This will be made possible through an upward trajectory of sales and profitable growth. Allowing us to, in turn, build upon our legacy as reliable healthcare partners.
LEADERSHIP QUOTE
“At USV, leadership means crafting a legacy of innovation, trust, and care.”

We are committed to advancing health and well-being, not just as a business, but as a purpose-driven partner to communities worldwide. Together, we lead with integrity, driven by our passion for excellence and a vision to create a healthier, sustainable future for all”
